Common questions

Where do the pipe sizing values come from?

The lookup is based on IPC Table 604.5 simplified guidance for water-supply branches on residential and light commercial work. Material capacity factors and pressure modifiers approximate typical conditions. For long runs with significant pressure loss, or commercial work with engineered plans, follow your engineered design rather than this app.

Why doesn't this account for length / fittings?

Friction loss from long runs and fittings is on the v1.1 roadmap. v1 is intended as a quick fixture-unit-to-pipe-size lookup, the way a plumber checks a chart on the truck.

Will there be drain sizing?

Planned as a sibling app — drain physics (gravity, slope) differ enough from supply sizing that it earns its own focused tool.

Will there be an Android version?

Planned. v1.x is iOS-only while we validate the design with real users.
