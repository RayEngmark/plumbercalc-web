Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

This app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

What we collect

Nothing. The app contains no analytics SDK, no advertising SDK, no crash reporter, no account system, and no network requests of any kind during normal use.

Data stored on your device

The app stores a single boolean on your device using iOS's local app storage to remember that you have acknowledged the educational-reference disclaimer. This value never leaves your phone. If you uninstall the app, this value is removed by iOS along with the app.

Permissions

The app does not request any iOS permissions.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect any data from any user, including those under 13. It complies with COPPA by collecting nothing.

Third-party services

None.

Changes to this policy

If the privacy posture of the app changes, this policy will be updated and the "Last updated" date will be revised.
